## ❓ Why I Built This

In our robotics club (IECRC), attendance was being managed manually, which was time-consuming and not very efficient. Maintaining records by hand also made it harder to track history and manage data properly.

To solve this, I decided to use my engineering skills to build a simple attendance system. The goal was to automate the process, reduce manual effort, and make attendance tracking more organized and reliable.

This project helps save time, keeps records structured, and makes the overall management of student attendance much easier.
